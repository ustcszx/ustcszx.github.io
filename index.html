About Me

I am a researcher currently affiliated with the University of Hong Kong (HKU), with an academic background rooted in extensive work at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC).

My research lies at the intersection of quantum physics and computer science. I am dedicated to exploring the fundamental limits of quantum simulation and developing variational algorithms that can be effectively implemented on near-term quantum devices. My work aims to bridge theoretical quantum concepts with practical applications in physics and computation.

You can follow my latest updates and thoughts on quantum computing on X (formerly Twitter) @zhongxiaquantum.
